The realm of artificial intelligence (AI) continues to push the boundaries of human imagination, unfolding a future where deepfake technology and personalized virtual assistants (PVAs) intertwine. The integration of deepfake technology into PVAs opens  up a new world of possibilities. Deepfake-powered  PVAs excel in recognizing and responding to users'  emotional states.
